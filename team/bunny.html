About me

I'm 19. I'd rather draw than talk most days, which is funny given my role on this team. The sketchbook is always somewhere on me. I notice the small stuff — the texture of bark, the way a stranger holds their coffee, a building's shadow at 4 PM. That's the stuff I want to put on a page.

What I do for HART

Community outreach, in my own way. I'm the one writing back to artists who DM us nervous about uploading their first piece. I read every single submission to the Voices wall. I help people figure out where they fit in this community when "fitting in" isn't really the goal — being seen is.

Mouse is the megaphone. I'm the inbox. Both are needed.

Why I'm here

Quiet artists get drowned out faster than anyone else, and they're often the ones who would benefit most from being seen. HART has a chance to be a space where you don't need to be loud to be respected. That's the part I want to protect.
